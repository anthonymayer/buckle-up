WELCOME TO BUCKLE UP!


Buckle Up! Is a new tool designed to help students privately identify their academic weaknesses based on real-time grade data, and discover online options for supplementing their education in these areas.


The Problem

The only access students have to their grades are snapshots of how they are doing at one instance of their entire academic career. This prevents students from correcting sub-satisfactory performance or from improving to increase their success in the long term. Additionally, finding online materials to supplement a student's coursework is increasingly difficult in an era of online education overload.



The Solution

    Show students their grade trends
    Automatically recommend additional, freely available, supplementary learning materials.
    Show true comparisons with other students at the class, grade, municipal, state, regional, or national levels.

These three pillars allow students to quickly identify and correct downward trends in order to increase their chances for success in their educational career, using free quality educational materials.

How It Works

    1. The student logs in and their grade/course data is pulled in via the LearnSprout API to show users their current performance.
    2. Negative trends are automatically identified and supplementary materials are suggested
    3. Students can access historical trends of their grades.
    4. Students can manipulate the charts to see how they compare with other populations at a given time or across a range of dates
